1) Click the Home tab in the top menu bar of Excel. 
2) Click Find & Select on the right side.
3) A pop-up box will appear. Select Blanks, and then hit OK.
Then Excel will then highlight all of the blank cells.
4) Once all the blank rows are highlighted, 
go to the Home tab and locate the Delete button on the right-hand side. 
5) Click the down arrow next to the Delete button and 
select Delete Sheet Rows to remove the blank rows.
